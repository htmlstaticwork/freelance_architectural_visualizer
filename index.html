Questions

Common Inquiries

How long does a typical render take?

Initial previews are delivered in 3-5 days. Final 4K renders usually take 7-10 business days depending on complexity.

What files do you need from my side?

We work best with .SKP, .FBX, or .RVV files. We also accept 2D PDF blueprints for full 3D modeling from scratch.

Do you offer revisions?

Each project includes two rounds of minor revisions to ensure the lighting and materiality perfectly match your vision.

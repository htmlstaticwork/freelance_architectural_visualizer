The Rise of Real-Time: UE5 in ArchViz

How real-time rendering is allowing architects to perform design reviews in live 3D environments.

Gone are the days of waiting 12 hours for a single frame to render. Unreal Engine 5's Lumen technology provides instant, cinematic-quality lighting updates, shifting the focus from 'waiting' to 'iterating'.

Zero Rendering Times

By moving to a real-time framework, we can produce high-quality cinematic animations in minutes rather than weeks, allowing for rapid design changes late in the project life cycle.

"Real-time isn't about speed; it's about the democratization of the design process."

The Collaborative Designer

Architects and developers can now meet in a live session, change daylight configurations, swap materials, and move walls instantly—all without leaving the headset or screen.
